I was recently admitted into UW as an Engineering Undeclared student,
and I plan to major in Electrical Engineering. My goal with this blog is
to document everything I do and all my achievement no matter how small or
simple. Everything I do is documented.

# The purpose behind my blog

I don't intend this to be ''look into my life'' type of blog, I will instead
document projects and things I learn such as C++ or a circuit that I built. I don't
expect **anyone** to read my blog, that's not the point. I instead want to keep
track of everything I do so that when it's time for an interview and the question of:

> Interviewer: "So Lyubomyr, your resume is all well and good, but what do you do
> and what have you done so far?"

I don't want to dread this question or stutter my way around as I struggle to remember
everything I done up to this point (and have an crisis when I realize I haven't done
anything!) Instead I want my response to be:

> Me: "Sure, let me pull up my blog where I document all my projects and achievements...
> Here it is, I even have a 'Currently Learning' section!"

That's the purpose of this blog

# Future projects

Right now I only just started to learn C++. My goal is to learn the basics in C++ and learn the
basics of Algorithms, in 3 months which is doable and about the length of one quarter course.
During that I will practice using LeetCode and do mini-projects, which I will document.

# Commitments

I want post at least once every week, deadline by Sunday (knowing how the brain works, I will post
only on Sundays). 

Keep a goal tab where I track my progress, and archive everything.

Have fun and focus on the process, not the destination.
